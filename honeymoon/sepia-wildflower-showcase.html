Inspiration

This palette captures the nostalgic beauty of wildflowers preserved in vintage photographs— colors softened by time and sun, evoking memories of golden summer days in mountain meadows. Each shade tells a story of natural beauty weathered by time, creating a palette that feels both timeless and deeply romantic.
