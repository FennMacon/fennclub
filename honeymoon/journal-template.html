Tuesday, July 1st, 2024
Grand Teton National Park, Wyoming

Today was absolutely magical! We woke up early for our river rafting adventure on the Snake River. The morning mist was still rising from the water as we paddled through the valley, with the Grand Tetons towering above us like ancient guardians.

But the real treasure was discovering the wildflower meadows afterward. We hiked through fields of lupines and Alpine Flame, their colors so vibrant against the mountain backdrop. I must have taken a hundred photos, trying to capture how the afternoon light made everything look like a vintage postcard - all sepia-toned and dreamy.

Day Highlights:
  • Snake River rafting at sunrise
  • Discovered the incredible wildflower meadows
  • Captured those perfect lupine & paintbrush photos
  • Evening campfire under the stars
  • Saw a moose drinking from the river!

The camping spot tonight is perfect - we can see the Milky Way from our tent. Tomorrow we head toward Moab, but I could stay in these mountains forever. There's something about the way the light hits the wildflowers that makes everything feel timeless and romantic.
